Trash Cash Android App

During the Capital One Software Engineering Summit Hackathon my team and I created an Android app called Trash Cash. The purpose of this app was to solve the issues of people not recycling. This app allows people to take a picture of an item that they are not sure is recyclable and then using the ML model Tensor flow, we were able to identify object that are recyclable and are not recyclable. Then once they take a photo of the recyclable item being thrown in the trash, money will go into their account for awarding themselves for recycling properly.
